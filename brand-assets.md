## 公司简介

STARK Environmental Solutions Ltd specializes in eco-friendly stainless steel water treatment products. We offer comprehensive services including R&D, manufacturing, sales, and after-sales support.

Our key products are mechanical filters, activated carbon filters, security filters, sterile water tanks, insulated water tanks, and mixing tanks, widely used in industries such as electronics, power plants, medicine, chemical industry, and food & beverage.

### 中文版本
STARK Environmental Solutions Ltd 专注于环保不锈钢水处理产品。我们提供研发、制造、销售和售后支持等综合服务。

我们的主要产品包括机械过滤器、活性炭过滤器、保安过滤器、无菌水箱、保温水箱和混合罐，广泛应用于电子、电厂、医药、化工和食品饮料等行业。

1. **Plate cutting**（板材切割）- 原材料按图纸切割
2. **Bending**（折弯）- 板材折弯或卷圆
